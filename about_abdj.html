A few years ago, during the pandemic, a project similar to this one was formerly made with the SuperColliderprogramming language. Afterwards, there was a urge to distribute it among people e.g. with Google Play, but SuperCollider is rather not suitable for building applications. Therefore, this project uses the JUCE library - it enables making apps including audio plugins for all commonly used operating systems!
